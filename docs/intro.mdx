## Getting Started

To get started with myTRS, you need to:

1. **Log in to your administrator account** at [admin.my-trs.com](https://admin.my-trs.com/administrators/sign_in)
2. **Navigate to your dashboard** to access all features
3. **Select the appropriate section** from the options above based on your needs
